I've also spent more time than I care to admit staring at lines of code, trying to debug the darn thing until it finally does what it's supposed to do. And when all else fails, I've learned to just hit it with a hammer (not really, but it's tempting). But hey, it's not all sparks and smoke. So, if you're in need of someone who knows their way around a circuit board and programming, or if you just want to geek out about the latest tech trends, you've come to the right place.
